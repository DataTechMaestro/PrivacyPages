Common questions

Does TraderCapital provide trading advice?

No. TraderCapital is for personal logging, journaling, and self-coaching only. It does not provide financial, investment, tax, legal, or trading advice. Past performance does not guarantee future results. Trading involves risk.

Does TraderCapital connect to my broker?

No. The app does not connect to brokerage accounts. You enter your own information manually.

Where is my data stored?

TraderCapital stores app data on your device. If iCloud is enabled, Apple may sync your data through your private iCloud account so it can appear on your other devices signed in with the same Apple ID.

Can I export my data?

The app includes CSV export so you can review activity history outside the app. Exported files are under your control once saved or shared.

Widgets look outdated or empty

Widgets show data saved by the app. Open TraderCapital and update your log or settings so the widget can refresh. You may need to remove and re-add the widget after large data resets.

How do I reset or delete my data?

In the app, go to Settings → Data & Reset for options to clear logs, rules, allocations, journal entries, settings, or all data at once.
