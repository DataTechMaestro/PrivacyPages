TraderCapital Privacy Policy

Effective date: 05/15/2026

TraderCapital is a personal trading journal, P/L tracker, and self-coaching tool. This policy explains what information the app handles and how it is used.

Information You Enter

TraderCapital lets you enter trading-related information such as daily profit/loss, rules, goals, funds, strategy criteria, notes, and activity history.

This information is used only to provide app features, including dashboards, calendars, widgets, progress tracking, exports, reminders, and iCloud sync.

Local Storage and iCloud Sync

TraderCapital stores your app data on your device. If iCloud is enabled, Apple may sync your TraderCapital data through your private iCloud account so it can be available on your devices.

TraderCapital does not operate a separate user account system or developer-run backend for your trading journal data.

Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking

TraderCapital does not currently include third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.

TraderCapital does not sell your data.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, TraderCapital uses local notification permissions to send reminders related to logging, rules, goals, and trading workflow. You can manage notification permissions in iOS Settings.

Widgets

TraderCapital widgets use shared app storage on your device to display recent app data, such as P/L progress, calendar summaries, and entry criteria.

Data Export and Deletion

TraderCapital may let you export data, such as CSV reports, for your own use. Exported files are controlled by you after they leave the app.

The app includes data reset controls for clearing app data. You can also delete the app from your device. iCloud-synced data may also need to be managed through your iCloud settings depending on Apple's sync behavior.

Financial Disclaimer

TraderCapital is for personal logging, journaling, and self-coaching only. It does not provide financial, investment, tax, legal, or trading advice. Past performance does not guarantee future results. Trading involves risk.
